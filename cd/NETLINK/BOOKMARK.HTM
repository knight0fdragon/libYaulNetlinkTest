Bookmarks



Your Bookmark page records the addresses of certain Web pages that were saved to the Sega Saturn's internal memory for quick access. Simply click on any one of the Web addresses and your Net Link browser will connect immediately to that Web page. You can reach your Bookmarks by pressing the B Button on the Sega Saturn Controller (or the Middle Button on the Mouse), or by selecting the Bookmarks Icon from the Toolbar. You can see more of your saved Bookmarks by clicking the Up and Down buttons on the lower right of the screen, or using the Left and Right Flipper controls.


You can add the current Web page's address by selecting Add Current.


If you want to remove one of your Bookmarks, point at the address and press the C Button. If you press the START or B Button you will put the Bookmarks away and return to your current Web page. Sega has pre-loaded some interesting and entertaining sites into the Bookmarks to get you started. You may delete any of these at any time.
