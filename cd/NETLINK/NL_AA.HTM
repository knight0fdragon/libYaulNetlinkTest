Choosing your own access......

In order to access the Internet, you will need to obtain the following information from your Internet Service Provider (ISP).

This list is also available in your NetLink manual, under "Using a Different Service Provider".
User Login
User Password
ISP Phone Number
DNS
Email Login
Email Password
Email Return Address
POP3 Server
SMTP Server
Please refer to the glossary section in your manual for details on items listed above or contact your Internet Service Provider (ISP).
