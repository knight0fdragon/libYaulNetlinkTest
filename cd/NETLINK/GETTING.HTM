Getting On The Web


In order to access the World Wide Web and use Electronic Mail, you will need an account with an Internet Service Provider (ISP). After you load the Net Link web browser for the first time, you will be taken through the account registration process. Have a valid credit card handy and follow the on-screen directions to make sure that all necessary information is recorded properly.


Starting an Internet Account with Mplayer Internet Service

  • Load your Net Link web browser (insert the CD and then turn on your Sega Saturn).

  • The web browser will automatically take you to the registration process.

  • Click on "Yes" when asked if you would like to sign up with Mplayer Internet Service.

  • To enter information in an available entry space, simply move your cursor with the D-pad on the Sega Saturn controller over the desired entry space and push the A Button. An on-screen keyboard will appear; move the cursor and select the desired characters by pushing the A Button on the Sega Saturn controller. As you select letters, you will see them appear in the selected entry space. If you make a mistake, click on the left facing arrow on the on-screen keyboard to erase the last character. Click on the "Enter" button when you are finished typing in characters for the selected entry space. Using the Z button on the controller will automatically advance you through the entry spaces.

  • Continue selecting entry spaces and entering your information until you have filled in all the necessary spaces.

  • Click on the "Submit" button when you are finished.

  • You will automatically be connected to Mplayer Internet Service's toll-free registration access line.

  • Continue to fill in the on-screen prompts as requested.


When you are finished, your Sega Saturn's memory will automatically be updated will all necessary information so you will not have to reenter it all again. However, we strongly recommend writing down all pertinent information in case your Sega Saturn's internal RAM is accidentally erased. Select "Options" from the Toolbar and then select "General Preferences," "Network Preferences," and "Mail Preferences" to bring up the information you should record in the manual.

Your credit card will be billed on a monthly basis. To cancel an account with Mplayer Internet Service, you will need to call their customer support center by telephone at (415)429-3100.


When you connect to the Internet, you will see a green box labelled "Modem Messages" appear. This is normal; the characters that appear are standard modem commands and will be used by yourself and Sega customer service to determine if your modem is dialing properly and connecting to your Internet Service Provider. Once connected, this box will disappear.


On your first connection to the Internet, you will see a simple instruction page on how to use the Net Link web browser and Net Link City interface. Please review this page carefully; if you have questions after seeing this page, please refer to this manual.
